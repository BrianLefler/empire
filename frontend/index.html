Welcome

Empire is a lightweight browser-based grand strategy game with asynchronous turn-based play. Each of the 26 players rules a nation and tries to achieve the goals they select. Game turns advance every few days, and players can set up their orders at any time between those advances.

Whether the game is cooperative or competitive is up to the players. Players are trying to beating national high scores from past games.
